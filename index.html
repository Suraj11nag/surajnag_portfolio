Hey, I'm Suraj 👋

I am a full-time data engineer and analyst who occasionally steps into the role of a data scientist when curiosity strikes. Guided by the philosophy, "An idiot admires complexity; a genius admires simplicity," I approach challenges with a focus on clarity and impact. Outside of data science and engineering, I enjoy writing for my blog, disconnecting to spend quality time offline

I’ve started breaking down data topics on my blog, making them simple, fun, and easy to understand. The internet and its amazing community taught me most of what I know, and I wouldn’t be here without it. Now, it’s my turn to give back—sharing what I’ve learned, one post at a time.

I see data science as a way to make sense of messy data. It’s about solving problems, trying new ideas, and improving existing methods to create something better.
