About Me

Since my childhood I was fascinated by maths, computers and technology. At 10, I got my first PC and I was curious enough to learn the complete microsoft office suite on my own. As a result I spent years of my childhood learning about them, creating interactive games using powerpoint & excel, fixing my computer (thanks to Windows XP and pesky computer viruses), I learnt the habbit of fixing errors, early in life. I even used to take apart hard drives just to see what was inside.

Other than writing blogs, I also enjoy spending my free time playing video games like Batman: Arkham Knight , Star Wars Jedi: Survivor etc.

I also build projects related to data science, data engineering, and machine learning. It's a hobby I picked up during my college days.

During my first job, I started writing blog where I write about my daily life, my failures, challenges etc. It has been a year and it always outgrows my fascination. I talk about a lot of things from my life, my perspective and my experiences.
