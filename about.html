About Me

Since childhood, I’ve been fascinated by math, computers, and technology. At 10, I got my first PC and taught myself Microsoft Office. I spent years experimenting—creating games with PowerPoint and Excel, fixing my computer (thanks to Windows XP), and developing a knack for troubleshooting. I even took apart hard drives just to see how they worked.

When I’m not writing blogs, I enjoy unwinding with video games like Batman: Arkham Knight , Star Wars Jedi: Survivor, Microsoft Flight Simulator, Chess etc.

I also build projects in data science, data engineering, and machine learning—a hobby I picked up back in college.

During my first job, I started a blog where I write about my daily life, failures, challenges, and more. A year later, it has outgrown my initial fascination. I share a variety of things—my perspective, experiences, and lessons along the way.
